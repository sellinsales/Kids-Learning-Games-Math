1. Who this app is for

Kids Learning Games: Math is a children's learning app designed for children and families. It includes multiple mini-games and activities focused on early learning, logic, memory, vocabulary, color recognition, and number skills.

2. What the app includes

The App may include one or more of the following learning and play modules:

  • Number Match and Missing Number activities
  • Math Challenges and counting practice
  • Vocabulary and word learning activities
  • Memory Puzzle and Pattern Match activities
  • Color Pop and color recognition activities
  • Science Facts and quiz-style learning activities
  • River Crossing and simple logic activities
  • Jungle Jumper, Room Cleanup, Fruit Slash, Galaxy Shooter, Neon Rush, Iso Adventure, and other child-friendly arcade mini-games
  • Quiz Battle or other local multiplayer-style educational modes, if available

This Privacy Policy applies to the full App experience and to all game modules included in the app unless a future version clearly states otherwise.

3. Information we collect

We may process the following types of information:

  • Game progress, settings, and preferences stored locally on the device.
  • Basic app interaction data needed to provide gameplay features.
  • Advertising-related data when ads are shown through Google AdMob or another approved ads provider.

We do not require players to create an account to use the App.

4. Local device storage

The App stores some information locally on the device, such as saved progress, sound settings, and gameplay preferences. This helps the App remember the player's experience.

5. Advertising

The App may show ads using Google AdMob or another Google Play compliant advertising provider. Ads providers may process certain information such as device identifiers, approximate device information, diagnostics, and ad performance data to serve and improve ads, prevent fraud, and measure results.

If the App is directed to children or mixed audiences, we intend to configure ads in a child-appropriate way and use only ad solutions that are permitted under applicable Google Play policies.

Learn more about how Google may process data: Google Privacy Policy
